Wooden performance

August 7, 2013
If an advertiser drops a ball in the forest, does it make a sound? This conundrum seems to have been cleared up by a Japanese mobile phone manufacturer. In this automated extravaganza, a tiny wooden ball descends a wooden ramp. The result is an amazing rendition of Bach’s Jesu, Joy of Man’s Desiring. All this to promote a kidney-shaped smartphone with the tagline, “Touch Wood.”
